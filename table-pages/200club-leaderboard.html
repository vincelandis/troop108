This award is presented annually to Scouts (youth and adult leaders) who have camped over night, continuously without a break with the troop or other scheduled Scouting campout events, including summer camp. An award trophy is presented to each Scout upon attaining 200 night camping streak at the annual fellowship banquet in February.
